<Warning>

</Warning>

## Product care and maintenance

When washed properly, microfiber bedding can last many washes. Follow these tips to maximize the life of your microfiber bedding.

## Washing

- Machine wash cold on permanent press or gentle cycle.
- Wash separately with like colors.
- Do not mix with terry or cotton sheets to avoid lint.

## Detergent

- Use a mild detergent.
- Do not bleach.
- Do not use fabric softener because it can pill fibers.

## Drying

- Tumble dry low and remove promptly.
- Do not iron or dry clean. High heat can melt fibers and make them rough.
