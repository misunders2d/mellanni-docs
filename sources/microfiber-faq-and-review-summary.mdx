## Customer-review summary

Customers find the microfiber sheets soft, comfortable, attractive, and a good value. They mention many color choices and say the sheets hold up well in the wash.

Fit feedback is mixed. Some customers say the fitted sheet stays in place, while others say it does not stay put.

Durability feedback is mixed. Some customers say the sheets last a long time, while others report ripping.
