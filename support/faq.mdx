<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do the supplied PDF, FAQ, and review notes apply to every Mellanni product?">
    No. The supplied PDF, product FAQ, and review summary are microfiber-only sources. Use product labels and live product pages for Egyptian cotton or other fabric families.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Which sheet set should I buy for a mattress up to 16 inches deep?">
    Choose the Iconic 4-Piece Microfiber Sheet Set. Its product pages list a deep-pocket design for mattresses up to 16" deep.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Which sheet set should I buy for a 17 to 21 inch mattress?">
    Choose an extra-deep microfiber sheet set or extra-deep fitted sheet. Mellanni lists those products for mattresses up to 21" thick.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What comes in the standard 4-piece set?">
    Most sizes include 1 fitted sheet, 1 flat sheet, and 2 pillowcases. Twin and Twin XL usually include 1 pillowcase. Split King listings include 2 fitted sheets.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What comes in the 6-piece extra-deep set?">
    The 6-piece extra-deep set includes 1 extra-deep fitted sheet, 1 flat sheet, and 4 envelope-style pillowcases.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How should I wash Mellanni microfiber sheets?">
    Machine wash cold on permanent press or gentle cycle. Use mild detergent and tumble dry low. Do not bleach, iron, dry clean, or use fabric softener.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What are Mellanni microfiber sheets made of?">
    The supplied product FAQ says the sheets are 100% brushed polyester microfiber. Mellanni product pages describe the Iconic collection as double-brushed microfiber.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What is the thread count?">
    Microfiber is measured in GSM, not thread count. The supplied product FAQ lists the sheets at 90-95 GSM.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Are these cotton sheets?">
    No. The supplied product FAQ says they are polyester microfiber, not cotton.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do fitted sheets stay in place?">
    The fitted sheet uses all-around elastic, but customer feedback on fit is mixed. Check mattress size and pocket depth before ordering. Use extra-deep products for 17" to 21" mattress stacks.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What do customers say most often?">
    The supplied review summary says customers most often mention softness, quality, appearance, value, comfort, fit, color, and durability. Fit and durability feedback is mixed. See [Customer questions and review themes](/support/customer-questions-and-review-themes).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why can sheet colors look different in person?">
    Colors can look different because displays, camera exposure, room lighting, time of day, wall color, and nearby bedding all affect how fabric reads. Nuanced non-neutral shades such as Spa Mint, Ivory, Terracotta, and Pistachio are especially hard to show exactly online.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Are the sheets OEKO-TEX certified?">
    The reviewed Iconic microfiber product pages list OEKO-TEX Standard 100 certification. Confirm the certificate number on the exact live product page.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Are Mellanni microfiber sheets hypoallergenic?">
    The Mellanni guide describes Mellanni microfiber sheets as hypoallergenic and suitable for sensitive skin. Use live product pages before adding hypoallergenic claims to SKU-specific packaging or retailer copy.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Should I use fabric softener?">
    No. The Mellanni washing guide says fabric softener and dryer sheets can coat microfiber and reduce moisture-wicking performance.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do Mellanni sheets have a lifetime warranty?">
    Mellanni's warranty page states that every product is backed by a lifetime warranty and that the Lifetime Warranty is valid within the USA.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Where can I buy Mellanni products?">
    The Mellanni guide lists mellanni.com, Amazon, Walmart, eBay, and Wayfair as official channels.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Does Mellanni ship outside the United States?">
    The reviewed shipping policy states that Mellanni does not ship internationally at this time.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I return or exchange a product?">
    Mellanni's returns page says customers can contact customer support for help with an exchange or refund. U.S. return shipping is listed as prepaid and free.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Are prices included in these docs?">
    No. Prices and discounts change often. Use live product pages for current pricing.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
