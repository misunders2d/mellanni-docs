## Product questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How deep is the fitted sheet?">
    The fitted sheet fits mattresses up to 16 inches high. The elastic runs all the way around the sheet — not just the corners — so it stays securely in place and won't slip off during the night.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do the sheets pill?">
    No. With normal use and proper care, Mellanni microfiber sheets are designed to resist pilling. Following the wash instructions on the tag keeps them looking and feeling their best.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do they feel slippery?">
    No. Mellanni sheets feel like high-quality cotton sheets that have been softened through many washes — not slippery like microfiber towels. Most customers are surprised by how similar the feel is to well-worn cotton.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do the sheets need ironing?">
    No ironing needed. After washing and drying, your sheets come out smooth and ready to use. The top sheet may have packaging creases when it first arrives, but those disappear after the first wash.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How breathable are Mellanni sheets?">
    Very breathable. Customers consistently report sleeping comfortably without getting too hot or too cold throughout the night.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I wash the sheets in hot water?">
    No. Machine wash cold with like colors. The care tag reads: "Machine wash cold with like colors. Do not bleach, dry clean or iron." Washing in hot water can damage the fabric and void your warranty coverage.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Will the sheets shrink?">
    Mellanni sheets are shrink-resistant and designed to maintain their size through repeated washes when you follow the cold-wash care instructions.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Ordering and shipping

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Where can I buy Mellanni products?">
    You can purchase Mellanni products through any of our official channels: mellanni.com, Amazon, Walmart, eBay, and Wayfair. Purchases from these channels are all covered by the Lifetime Satisfaction Warranty.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you ship internationally?">
    Not at this time. Mellanni ships to US addresses only, including APO/FPO addresses and US territories.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long does shipping take?">
    Orders are processed in 1–3 business days and then shipped with a delivery window of 3–8 business days. You will receive a tracking number once your order ships.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
