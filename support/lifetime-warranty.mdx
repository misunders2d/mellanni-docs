Every Mellanni product you purchase from an official channel is covered by our Lifetime Satisfaction Warranty. If you are ever unsatisfied for any reason — whether your sheets ripped, the color wasn't what you expected, or you simply don't like them — we will make it right. With over 300,000 five-star reviews, we stand behind every product we sell. This warranty is valid for purchases made within the USA.

## Official channels

The Lifetime Satisfaction Warranty applies to purchases made through the following channels:

- mellanni.com
- Amazon
- Walmart
- eBay
- Wayfair

## What's covered

There is no fine print. If you are unsatisfied for **any** reason, you can choose one of the following resolutions:

- **Replacement** — we ship you the same product at no cost
- **Full refund** — returned to your original payment method
- **In-store credit** — applied to a future Mellanni purchase

## How to register your product

You must register your product to activate the Lifetime Satisfaction Warranty.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the registration page">
    Go to [mellanni.com/pages/lifetime-warranty](https://mellanni.com/pages/lifetime-warranty).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your purchase details">
    Provide your order number, the marketplace you purchased from, and your product information.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit your registration">
    Complete the form to activate your warranty and receive confirmation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Register your product immediately after purchase so your warranty is active before you ever need it.
</Tip>

<Note>
  When you register, you also join the **Mellanni VIP Club**. Members receive exclusive coupons, member-only discounts, and opportunities to receive free products.
</Note>

## How to file a claim

<Steps>
  <Step title="Gather your order details">
    Before contacting support, have the following ready:

    - Marketplace you purchased from (e.g., Amazon, mellanni.com)
    - Order number
    - Date of purchase
    - Product type, size, and color
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact Mellanni support">
    Submit your claim at [mellanni.com/pages/contact-us-new](https://mellanni.com/pages/contact-us-new). Include all the details from the previous step so our team can process your request quickly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your resolution">
    Once your claim is reviewed, select your preferred resolution: a replacement, a full refund, or in-store credit.
  </Step>
</Steps>
