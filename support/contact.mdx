The Mellanni support team is here to help you with any question or issue — from warranty claims and returns to damaged shipments and order tracking. You can reach us through the contact form, social media, or our specialized inquiry pages listed below.

<Tip>
  The Mellanni support team responds to all inquiries within 24 hours. For the fastest resolution, include your order details in your first message.
</Tip>

<Note>
  Before reaching out, have the following information ready:

  - The marketplace you purchased from (e.g., Amazon, mellanni.com)
  - Your order number
  - Date of purchase
  - Product type, size, and color
  - For damaged or wrong items: photos of the packaging and the product
  - For lost packages: your tracking number
</Note>

## Returns and exchanges

To return or exchange a product, contact our support team through the [general contact form](https://mellanni.com/pages/contact-us). Once your request is reviewed, we will send you a prepaid return label — you won't pay anything out of pocket to ship it back.

## Damaged or incorrect items

If your order arrived damaged or you received the wrong item, contact us at [mellanni.com/pages/contact-us](https://mellanni.com/pages/contact-us) and include:

- Photos of the packaging (exterior)
- Photos of the product
- Your Order ID

This helps us resolve the issue as quickly as possible.

## Lost packages

If your tracking shows your package as delivered but you haven't received it, contact us with your tracking number and we will look into it right away.
