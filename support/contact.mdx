Contact Mellanni support when you need help with an order, return, exchange, warranty claim, damaged item, wrong item, or lost package.

## Contact details

| Channel | Detail |
| --- | --- |
| Contact form | [mellanni.com/pages/contact-us](https://mellanni.com/pages/contact-us) |
| Warranty claim form | [mellanni.com/pages/contact-us-new](https://mellanni.com/pages/contact-us-new) |
| Email listed on Mellanni site | support@mellanni.com |
| Phone listed on Mellanni site | (888) 995-1195 |
| Business hours listed on Mellanni site | Monday-Friday, 7 AM-8 PM EST |

<Tip>
  The Mellanni guide says support responds within 24 hours. Include complete order details in the first message to reduce back-and-forth.
</Tip>

## Include this information

- Marketplace or site where you purchased.
- Order number.
- Purchase date.
- Product type, size, and color.
- Tracking number for delivery issues.
- Photos of packaging and product for damaged, defective, wrong, or missing-item claims.
