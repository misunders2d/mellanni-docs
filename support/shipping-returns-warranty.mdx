This page summarizes Mellanni support policies from the shipping, returns, and warranty pages.

## Shipping

| Topic | Detail |
| --- | --- |
| Standard U.S. shipping | Free standard shipping for orders shipped within the United States |
| APO/FPO/DPO and U.S. territories | Free shipping is listed as available |
| International shipping | Not available at the time of the reviewed policy page |
| Processing time | 1-3 business days |
| Standard delivery time | Most standard shipments arrive in 3-8 business days after shipping |
| Tracking | Sent by email after shipment |
| Official channels | The Mellanni guide lists mellanni.com, Amazon, Walmart, eBay, and Wayfair |

## Expedited shipping

Mellanni lists expedited options for U.S. orders at checkout. The reviewed policy page lists:

| Option | Listed cost | Listed delivery window |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Priority Shipping | $13.99 | Up to 3 business days, excluding processing time |
| Expedited Shipping | $5.49 | Up to 5 business days, excluding processing time |

<Info>
  Expedited shipping costs are listed as non-refundable. Confirm checkout pricing before quoting expedited rates.
</Info>

## Returns and exchanges

Mellanni says customers can contact support for an exchange or refund if a product does not meet expectations. If a product is determined defective due to materials or workmanship, Mellanni lists replacement or refund as available outcomes.

Return details from the reviewed policy pages:

- Contact Mellanni for return instructions.
- Mellanni says it responds with further instructions within 24 hours.
- Return shipping is listed as prepaid and free for U.S.-based customers.
- Refunds are listed as processed within 1-5 business days.
- Depending on marketplace and payment method, refunds can appear within 1-10 business days.

## Lifetime warranty

Mellanni states that every product is backed by a lifetime warranty. The warranty page lists these possible claim outcomes:

- Replacement.
- Refund to the original payment method.
- In-store credit.

Mellanni asks warranty claimants to provide:

- Marketplace where the product was purchased.
- Order number.
- Purchase date.
- Product type, size, and color.

<Warning>
  Mellanni's warranty page states that the Lifetime Warranty is only valid within the USA.
</Warning>

## Product registration

The Mellanni warranty guide says product registration activates the Lifetime Satisfaction Warranty and references the registration page at [mellanni.com/pages/lifetime-warranty](https://mellanni.com/pages/lifetime-warranty). It also says registration adds the customer to the Mellanni VIP Club.

<Info>
  Mellanni's own warranty page says registration can be completed even without an order number, because the team can match the order by email. Use the live warranty page for the current form requirements.
</Info>
