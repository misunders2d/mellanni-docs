Use this page to answer high-volume customer questions about Mellanni microfiber sheets without turning review summaries into product guarantees. The question list comes from the supplied product FAQ section. The review themes come from an AI-generated customer-review summary supplied with the project materials.

<Note>
  The supplied FAQ, review summary, and care PDF are microfiber-only sources. They do not apply to Egyptian cotton, duvet covers, or other non-microfiber products unless those product pages confirm the same details.
</Note>

## Top question themes

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What are the sheets made of?">
    The supplied product FAQ describes the sheets as 100% brushed polyester microfiber. Mellanni product pages describe the Iconic collection as double-brushed microfiber.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What is the thread count?">
    Thread count is not the right measurement for microfiber. The supplied product FAQ says microfiber is measured in GSM and lists the sheets at 90-95 GSM.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Are these cotton sheets?">
    No. The supplied product FAQ says these are 100% polyester microfiber sheets, not cotton sheets.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Are chemicals used in production?">
    Mellanni product pages list OEKO-TEX Standard 100 certification for reviewed Iconic microfiber products. That certification indicates testing against over 1,000 substances. See [Certification and safety](/trust/certification-and-safety).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How deep are the fitted-sheet pockets?">
    Standard Iconic microfiber fitted sheets are listed for mattresses up to 16" deep. Extra-deep sets and fitted-sheet separates are listed for mattresses up to 21" deep.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What comes in the Queen set?">
    The supplied product FAQ lists the Queen set as 1 fitted sheet, 1 flat sheet, and 2 pillowcases. The cited dimensions are 80" x 60" for the fitted sheet, 102" x 90" for the flat sheet, and 20" x 30" for each pillowcase.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Will they fit a memory foam mattress or topper?">
    They can fit memory foam mattresses up to the listed pocket depth. Measure the mattress and topper together. Use the standard set up to 16" and the extra-deep collection for 17" to 21".
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do fitted sheets stay on the mattress?">
    The fitted sheet uses all-around elastic. Customer feedback on fit is mixed: some customers say the fitted sheet stays in place, while others say it does not stay put. Use the correct pocket depth and tuck the corners fully under the mattress.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I machine wash them?">
    Yes. The supplied care PDF says to machine wash cold on permanent press or gentle cycle.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I tumble dry them?">
    Yes. Tumble dry low and remove promptly.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Will they shrink?">
    Mellanni product pages describe the sheets as shrink resistant. Use cold water and low heat to protect fit.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do they wrinkle?">
    Mellanni product pages describe the sheets as wrinkle resistant. Customer question content says most customers say they come out smooth.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do they fade?">
    Mellanni product pages describe the sheets as fade resistant. Wash with like colors and avoid bleach.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Are they cooling sheets?">
    The supplied FAQ describes them as lightweight and breathable. Some hot sleepers still report warmth, so do not position microfiber as guaranteed cooling bedding.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Are they good for all seasons?">
    The supplied FAQ describes them as temperature-regulating. Customer comfort depends on room temperature, sleep style, mattress protector, and blanket layers.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How soft are they?">
    Softness is the strongest review theme in the supplied summary, with 50.2K mentions. The FAQ screenshot says 96% of reviewers rate softness positively.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What colors are available?">
    The supplied FAQ and Mellanni guide describe 40+ colors and patterns. Confirm current color availability on the live product page.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Are the colors true to the photos?">
    Customer feedback is mixed. The supplied FAQ says some customers report colors run slightly darker than shown. Colors can also shift across displays and lighting conditions, especially nuanced non-neutral shades such as Spa Mint, Ivory, Terracotta, and Pistachio.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Were these featured on the Today Show?">
    The supplied FAQ says yes. Use a verified press source before adding this claim to public marketing pages.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is there a warranty?">
    Mellanni's warranty page describes the Mellanni Lifetime Promise. See [Lifetime warranty](/support/lifetime-warranty).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Customer-review themes

These themes apply to the supplied microfiber review summary.

| Theme | Mentions in supplied summary | What customers say |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| Softness | 50.2K | Customers often describe the sheets as very soft and luxurious. |
| Quality | 48.4K | Many customers describe the sheets as good quality, especially for routine washing. |
| Appearance | 28.8K | Customers mention the sheets look beautiful on the bed. |
| Value for money | 22.9K | Customers often describe the set as a good value. |
| Comfort | 19.9K | Customers commonly describe the sheets as comfortable. |
| Fit | 17.7K | Feedback is mixed. Some customers say the fitted sheet stays in place, while others say it does not stay put. |
| Color | 12.2K | Customers value the broad color range. |
| Durability | 9.9K | Feedback is mixed. Some customers say the sheets last a long time, while others report ripping. |

## How to use these themes

- Lead support answers with verified product specs before customer sentiment.
- Treat review themes as expectation-setting, not as a promise.
- Mention mixed fit feedback when helping customers choose between standard and extra-deep pockets.
- Mention mixed durability feedback with care guidance: wash cold, use mild detergent, avoid fabric softener, avoid lint-heavy loads, and tumble dry low.
