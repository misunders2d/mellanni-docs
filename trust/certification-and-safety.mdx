Mellanni product pages state that the reviewed Iconic microfiber sheet products are OEKO-TEX Standard 100 certified.

## What the product pages say

The product pages state that each component of the certified product has been tested against a list of over 1,000 substances. They also point customers to the OEKO-TEX Label Check for verification.

## Certificate numbers found on product pages

| Product page | Certificate number listed |
| --- | --- |
| Standard Iconic 4-Piece Microfiber Sheet Set | 22.HUS.40891 |
| Extra-deep 4-piece light-color listing | 22.HUS.40981 |
| Extra-deep fitted sheet dark-color listing | 22.HUS.40981 |

<Info>
  Certificate numbers can vary by product, color family, or page version. Verify the number on the current live product page before publishing packaging, compliance, or retailer copy.
</Info>

## Verification workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the live product page">
    Use the exact SKU or color-family product page you plan to reference.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the Certification section">
    Confirm whether the page lists OEKO-TEX Standard 100 and note the certificate number.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check the OEKO-TEX label">
    Use the OEKO-TEX Label Check linked from the product page to verify validity.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Document the source">
    Record the product URL, certificate number, and verification date in internal records.
  </Step>
</Steps>
