Fitted-sheet separates are for replacing the bottom sheet without buying a full sheet set. Mellanni lists standard fitted sheets and extra-deep fitted sheets as separate products.

## Choose the right fitted sheet

| Product | Use when | Pocket depth |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Iconic Microfiber Fitted Sheet | You need a replacement fitted sheet for a standard mattress | Standard deep-pocket fit, with sizes listed by mattress dimensions |
| Iconic Microfiber Extra-Deep Fitted Sheet | You need a replacement fitted sheet for a tall mattress or topper setup | Fits mattresses up to 21" thick |

## Fit checklist

<Steps>
  <Step title="Measure width and length">
    Match the mattress size first: Twin, Twin XL, Full, Queen, King, or California King.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Measure height">
    Include toppers and pads. If the total height is more than 16", use an extra-deep fitted sheet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check corners after one night">
    If the fitted sheet pulls up, wrinkles heavily at the corners, or strains the elastic, move to a deeper pocket.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Care summary

Machine wash cold and tumble dry low. Do not bleach or iron.
