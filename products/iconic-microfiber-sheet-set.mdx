The Iconic 4-Piece Microfiber Sheet Set is the standard Mellanni sheet set for most everyday mattresses. It uses deep-pocket fitted-sheet construction for mattresses up to 16" deep and includes a fitted sheet, a flat sheet, and pillowcases.

![White Mellanni Iconic microfiber sheet set](https://cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/2725/6308/files/BED-SHEET-SET-WHITE_1.jpg?v=1744384364)

## Use this set when

- The mattress is 16" deep or less.
- You want one fitted sheet, one flat sheet, and pillowcases in one purchase.
- You want a low-maintenance microfiber sheet set for repeated household washing.
- You want a broad range of solid colors, patterns, and seasonal colors.
- You want the color range documented by Mellanni as 40+ options.

<Warning>
  Do not choose the standard set for 17" to 21" mattresses unless the specific product page says that variant is designed for that depth. Use the extra-deep collection instead.
</Warning>

## Buying notes

- Choose standard sizes by mattress width and length first.
- Use pocket depth as a separate check after choosing size.
- Choose Split King only for split adjustable bases that use two Twin XL mattresses.
- Pillowcase count changes by size: Twin and Twin XL usually include 1 pillowcase; larger sizes include 2.
- Pillowcase and sheet colors can be mixed with separate pillowcase purchases when exact color and fabric family match.
- Customer review summaries are positive on softness, comfort, appearance, value, and color range. Fit and durability feedback is mixed, so measure the mattress stack and follow the care guide closely.

## Care summary

Wash with cold water and tumble dry low. Do not bleach, iron, or dry clean. See [Care and laundry](/guides/care-and-laundry) for the full workflow.
