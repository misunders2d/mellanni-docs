## Fit

Extra-deep fitted sheets are described as fitting mattresses up to 21" thick. The fitted sheet uses all-around elastic to help keep the sheet in place.

Choose extra-deep when the bed includes:

- 17", 18", 19", 20", or 21" mattress height.
- Pillow-top or double pillow-top construction.
- Memory foam topper.
- Thick mattress pad.
- Air mattress or waterbed mattress.
- Adjustable bed.

<Tip>
  If the mattress is exactly 16", choose based on corner behavior. If a standard 16" fitted sheet stays tucked after sleep, the standard set is enough. If corners lift or the sheet feels strained, move to extra-deep.
</Tip>
