Mellanni's bedding lineup centers on sheet sets, fitted-sheet separates, pillowcases, and duvet cover sets. The Gladiator Mintlify guide also documents the brand's Egyptian cotton option alongside the double-brushed microfiber collection.

## Product categories

| Category | What it includes | Key details |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Sheet sets | Fitted sheet, flat sheet, and pillowcases | Standard deep-pocket and extra-deep options |
| Fitted sheets | One fitted sheet | Useful for replacing only the bottom sheet |
| Pillowcases | Pillowcase sets and separates | Envelope closure, no buttons or zippers |
| Duvet cover sets | Duvet cover and matching shams | Button closure on the duvet cover |

## Fabric options

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Double-brushed microfiber">
    Mellanni's Iconic collection uses double-brushed microfiber. It is positioned as soft, moisture-wicking, wrinkle resistant, and easy to care for.

    | Attribute | Detail |
    | --- | --- |
    | Feel | Smooth and soft from first use |
    | Care | Machine wash cold, tumble dry low |
    | Strengths | Fade, stain, shrink, pill, and wrinkle resistance |
    | Best for | Everyday homes, guest rooms, dorms, vacation rentals, and low-maintenance bedding rotations |
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Egyptian cotton">
    The Gladiator guide describes Mellanni's Egyptian cotton option as a natural-fiber choice that softens over time and suits sleepers who prefer a cotton feel.

    | Attribute | Detail |
    | --- | --- |
    | Feel | Crisp at first, softer with washing |
    | Care | Follow the specific product label |
    | Strengths | Natural breathability and cotton hand feel |
    | Best for | Sleepers who prefer natural fibers or a more classic cotton sheet |
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
