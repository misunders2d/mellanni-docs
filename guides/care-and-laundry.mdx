Mellanni product pages list the same core care instructions across Iconic microfiber sheet sets: machine wash cold, tumble dry low, do not bleach, and do not iron. The Gladiator guide adds practical care guidance for lint control, first wash, fabric softener, and storage.

## Standard care

<Steps>
  <Step title="Wash with cold water">
    Use cold water and wash with similar colors.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Keep microfiber away from lint-heavy fabrics">
    Wash microfiber sheets separately or with similar smooth fabrics. Avoid towels, fleece, denim, and rough items that shed lint or increase abrasion.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use a gentle detergent">
    Use regular laundry detergent. Avoid bleach, fabric softener, and dryer sheets.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Dry on low heat">
    Tumble dry low. Remove promptly when dry to reduce wrinkles.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fold or remake the bed">
    Smooth the fitted sheet by hand while placing it on the mattress.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What to avoid

| Avoid | Reason |
| --- | --- |
| Bleach | Product pages say not to bleach |
| Ironing | Product pages say not to iron |
| High heat drying | Low heat is the listed drying instruction |
| Dry cleaning | FAQ content references tags that say not to dry clean |
| Fabric softener or dryer sheets | Gladiator guide says they can coat microfiber and reduce moisture-wicking performance |
| Overloading washer or dryer | Crowding can increase wrinkling and uneven drying |

<Tip>
  For fewer wrinkles, move sheets from washer to dryer soon after the cycle ends, dry on low, and remove when dry instead of leaving them in a hot dryer drum.
</Tip>

## Stains and repeated washing

The product pages describe the sheets as stain, fade, shrink, pill, and wrinkle resistant. Treat stains quickly and avoid high heat until the stain is removed, because heat can set some stains into fabric.

## Pilling and texture

Pilling can be affected by friction, wash load, detergent amount, and dryer heat. To reduce abrasion:

- Wash sheets with bedding or other soft textiles.
- Avoid washing with rough garments, hook-and-loop closures, or heavy zippers.
- Do not overload the washer.
- Use low heat in the dryer.
- Avoid fabric softener and dryer sheets.

## Storage

Store sheets dry and folded. If storing a full set, place the fitted sheet and flat sheet inside one pillowcase so the set stays together.

The Gladiator guide also recommends storing clean, fully dry sheets in a cool, dry place and using the included storage bag when available.
