Choose sheet size in two passes: mattress footprint first, pocket depth second.

## Standard mattress sizes

| Mattress size | Fitted sheet size | Common pillowcase size |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Twin | 39" x 75" | Standard, 20" x 30" |
| Twin XL | 39" x 80" | Standard, 20" x 30" |
| Full | 54" x 75" | Standard, 20" x 30" |
| Queen | 60" x 80" | Standard, 20" x 30" |
| King | 78" x 80" | King, 20" x 40" |
| California King | 72" x 84" | King, 20" x 40" |
| Split King | Two 39" x 80" fitted sheets | King, 20" x 40" |

<Info>
  Mellanni product pages and the Mellanni guide can list mattress and fitted-sheet dimensions in slightly different width conventions, such as 38" or 39" for Twin and Twin XL. Match the product variant to the named mattress size and confirm dimensions on the live product page.
</Info>

## Pocket depth

| Mattress height | Recommended collection | Why |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Up to 16" | Iconic 4-Piece Microfiber Sheet Set | Standard deep-pocket design is listed for mattresses up to 16" deep |
| 17" to 21" | Extra-deep microfiber sheet set or extra-deep fitted sheet | Extra-deep products are listed for mattresses up to 21" thick |
| More than 21" | Verify before purchase | Mellanni product pages reviewed here list up to 21" for extra-deep products |

## Measure the bed

<Steps>
  <Step title="Remove loose bedding">
    Leave the mattress pad and topper in place if they stay on the bed under the fitted sheet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Measure width">
    Measure side to side across the mattress surface.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Measure length">
    Measure head to foot across the mattress surface.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Measure height">
    Measure from the bottom edge of the mattress stack to the top sleeping surface.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check the corners">
    If the corner is rounded, pillow-topped, or compressed by a pad, use the highest practical point for the depth measurement.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Common fit scenarios

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Standard Queen mattress">
    Choose Queen in the Iconic 4-Piece Microfiber Sheet Set when the total mattress height is 16" or less.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Queen mattress with topper">
    Measure the mattress and topper together. If the total height is 17" to 21", choose Queen in the extra-deep collection.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Adjustable Split King base">
    Choose Split King only when the bed uses two Twin XL mattresses. The Split King listing includes two fitted sheets.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Pillow-top mattress">
    Use extra-deep if the pillow-top layer makes the total height greater than 16" or if standard fitted-sheet corners lift.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Decision guide

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Standard pocket" icon="check" type="check">
    Use for mattresses up to 16" deep and most conventional bed setups.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Extra-deep pocket" icon="move-vertical" type="tip">
    Use for 17" to 21" mattresses, thick toppers, pillow tops, mattress pads, and adjustable beds.
  </Card>
</Columns>
