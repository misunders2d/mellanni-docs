Mellanni lists Iconic microfiber sheets across light colors, dark colors, new colors, solid colors, and patterns. The Gladiator guide describes the sheet range as 40+ colors. Exact availability changes by size and product listing.

## Choose by room use

| Room | Recommended approach |
| --- | --- |
| Primary bedroom | Start with a neutral set, then add a seasonal color as a second set |
| Guest bedroom | Choose easy-to-match neutrals such as white, ivory, gray, or beige |
| Kids room | Use darker colors or patterns when visible wear is a concern |
| Split King adjustable bed | Choose colors from listings that explicitly include Split King |

## Coordinate sets and separates

When replacing only one piece, match three details:

1. Product family: standard or extra-deep.
2. Size: Twin, Full, Queen, King, and so on.
3. Color name on the live listing.

<Warning>
  Similar color names can look different across production runs, product pages, or screen settings. For exact visual matching, buy replacement pieces from the same product family and verify the live color swatch.
</Warning>

## Pattern notes

Pattern listings can have a smaller size range than solid-color listings. If a pattern is not available in the needed size, choose a solid color that coordinates with the duvet cover, blanket, or pillow shams.

## Availability notes

- Some colors are sold across both standard and extra-deep collections.
- Some colors appear only in light-color, dark-color, new-color, or last-chance listings.
- Some variants may be sold out while nearby sizes or colors remain available.

## Care impact by color

| Color family | What to expect |
| --- | --- |
| Dark colors | Can show lint and light pet hair more clearly. Wash separately from lint-producing fabrics. |
| Light colors | Can show stains more clearly. Treat stains quickly with cold water. |
| Mid-tones | Often more forgiving for everyday use, guest rooms, and rentals. |
