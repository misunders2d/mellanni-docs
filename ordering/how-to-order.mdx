Mellanni products are available through mellanni.com and official retail channels referenced by the Mellanni guide: Amazon, Walmart, eBay, and Wayfair.

## Order from mellanni.com

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse collections">
    Start from the Mellanni navigation or product collections. For sheets, use Sheet Sets, Fitted & Flat Sheets, or Pillowcases.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose size and color">
    Select the size first, then confirm color availability. Color, size, and stock can vary by product page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm pocket depth">
    Use standard deep-pocket sheets for mattresses up to 16" deep. Use extra-deep sheets for mattresses up to 21" thick.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add to cart">
    Review item name, size, color, quantity, and price before checkout.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter shipping and payment">
    Confirm the shipping address is complete. The Mellanni guide lists Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, Diners Club, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, Amazon Pay, and Shop Pay as accepted payment options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save order details">
    Keep the confirmation email. You may need the order number for tracking, returns, exchanges, or warranty support.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Same-day changes and cancellations

Mellanni's shipping policy says there is only a brief window before orders are processed and sent to the shipping dock. Contact support the same day if you need to:

- Correct a shipping address or recipient name.
- Change product color or quantity.
- Add, remove, or change products.
- Cancel the order.

<Warning>
  Once the order has shipped, Mellanni says delivery cannot be stopped. If you decide not to keep the product, follow the return process for unopened products.
</Warning>

## Different billing and shipping addresses

If the billing and shipping addresses differ, the shipping policy says the order may be flagged for fraud review. Contact support after placing the order to reduce processing delays.
