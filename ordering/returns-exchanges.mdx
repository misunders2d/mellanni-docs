Mellanni makes it easy to return or exchange any product you are not completely satisfied with. You can reach the customer support team by email through [mellanni.com/pages/contact-us](https://mellanni.com/pages/contact-us), and the team will get back to you within 24 hours to help you choose the best resolution. US customers receive a free prepaid return shipping label, so there is no upfront postage cost.

## Resolution options

When you contact support, you can choose from three resolutions:

| Option | What you receive |
|---|---|
| Exchange | A replacement product sent to you |
| In-store credit | Credit applied to your Mellanni account for a future purchase |
| Full refund | Refund to your original payment method |

If your product has a defect caused by improper materials or workmanship, you are entitled to a replacement or a full refund.

## How to start a return or exchange

<Steps>
  <Step title="Contact customer support by email">
    Visit [mellanni.com/pages/contact-us](https://mellanni.com/pages/contact-us) and send a message describing the issue with your order. Include your order number and any relevant details or photos.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive your prepaid return label">
    The support team will reply within 24 hours and provide a free prepaid return shipping label. No postage cost is required from you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pack and ship your item">
    Pack the item securely and attach the prepaid label. Drop the package off at the carrier location specified on the label.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive your resolution">
    Once Mellanni receives your return, your refund, exchange, or in-store credit will be processed within 1–5 business days. The amount will appear in your account within 1–10 business days, depending on your marketplace and payment method.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  If you are ever unsatisfied with a Mellanni product — even after extended use — Mellanni's Lifetime Warranty covers you. Contact customer support at any time to request a replacement or refund under the warranty.
</Note>

<Warning>
  Expedited shipping charges (Priority Shipping or Expedited Shipping) are non-refundable, regardless of the reason for the return.
</Warning>
