Mellanni offers three core product categories — sheet sets, pillowcases, and duvet cover sets — all designed to work together and available in a wide range of colors and sizes. Every item is hypoallergenic and machine washable, making them practical for everyday use as well as guest rooms, vacation rentals, and college dorms.

## Sheet sets

Mellanni's [Microfiber 1800 Series sheet sets](https://mellanni.com/collections/bed-sheets) are the cornerstone of the lineup. Each set includes a fitted sheet, flat sheet, and the matching number of pillowcases for your chosen size. The fitted sheet features a full-perimeter elastic band — not just corner pockets — and deep pockets that accommodate mattresses up to 16 inches thick, including pillow-top and memory foam styles.

**Available sizes:** Twin, Twin XL, Full, Queen, King, California King, Split King

**Colors:** 40+ options to match any bedroom palette

**Key features:**
- Double-brushed microfiber construction
- Fade, stain, shrink, wrinkle, and pill resistant
- Hypoallergenic and suitable for sensitive skin

## Pillowcases

[Mellanni pillowcases](https://mellanni.com/collections/pillowcases) are available in Standard, Queen, and King sizes and feature an envelope closure that keeps your pillow securely in place through the night. They match the full sheet set color range so you can mix, coordinate, or replace individual cases without buying a whole new set.

**Available sizes:** Standard, Queen, King

**Closure:** Envelope-style (no buttons or zippers)

## Duvet cover sets

[Mellanni duvet cover sets](https://mellanni.com/collections/duvet-cover-sets) include the duvet cover and matching shams. The cover uses a button closure along the bottom edge to keep your insert in place, and the entire set is machine washable on a gentle cycle. Sizes correspond to standard comforter and duvet insert dimensions.

**Included:** Duvet cover + shams

**Closure:** Button closure

## Microfiber vs. Egyptian cotton

Mellanni offers two material choices. Use the tabs below to compare them and decide which suits your sleep style.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Double-brushed microfiber">
    Mellanni's double-brushed 100% microfiber is the material behind the award-winning 1800 Series. The brushing process creates an exceptionally smooth hand feel while making the fabric highly durable and easy to care for.

    | Feature | Detail |
    |---|---|
    | Material | 100% microfiber |
    | Feel | Silky, ultra-soft |
    | Temperature | Cool and moisture-wicking |
    | Breathability | Good — draws moisture away from skin |
    | Wrinkle resistance | Excellent — resists wrinkles right out of the dryer |
    | Durability | Fade, stain, shrink, and pill resistant |
    | Hypoallergenic | Yes |
    | Care | Machine wash cold, tumble dry low |

    **Best for:** Sleepers who want easy-care sheets that feel soft immediately, hold their color wash after wash, and work in any season.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Egyptian cotton">
    Mellanni's Egyptian Cotton option is made from long-staple natural fibers that grow softer with every wash. Cotton breathes naturally and regulates temperature more actively than synthetic fabrics.

    | Feature | Detail |
    |---|---|
    | Material | 100% Egyptian cotton |
    | Feel | Crisp initially, softens over time |
    | Temperature | Naturally breathable and temperature-regulating |
    | Breathability | Excellent — ideal for warm sleepers |
    | Wrinkle resistance | Moderate — may benefit from a quick iron |
    | Durability | Long-lasting with proper care |
    | Hypoallergenic | Yes |
    | Care | Machine wash warm, tumble dry medium |

    **Best for:** Sleepers who run warm, prefer natural fibers, or want sheets that continue to improve with age.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
