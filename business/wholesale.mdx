Mellanni welcomes retailers, hospitality operators, healthcare facilities, and any large-volume buyer looking for reliable, high-quality bedding at competitive prices. Whether you run a hotel, vacation rental, hospital, retail store, or corporate gifting program, you can reach out to the wholesale team to discuss pricing and quantities tailored to your needs.

## Who wholesale is for

Wholesale and bulk pricing is available to a wide range of buyers, including:

- Hotels and resorts
- Hospitals and healthcare facilities
- Vacation rental operators
- Retail stores carrying bedding products
- Corporate gifting programs
- Any business or organization placing large-volume orders

## How to start a wholesale inquiry

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the wholesale page">
    Go to [mellanni.com/pages/wholesale-bulk](https://mellanni.com/pages/wholesale-bulk) to access the inquiry form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill out the inquiry form">
    Complete the form with details about your company and order needs. Include as much information as possible to help the team prepare a tailored quote.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for the team to reach out">
    A Mellanni representative will contact you to discuss pricing, minimums, and terms.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  The Mellanni wholesale team responds to all inquiries in less than 24 hours to begin negotiating terms.
</Note>

## What to include in your inquiry

Providing complete details in your initial inquiry helps the team respond with an accurate quote faster.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Company name" icon="building">
    Your business or organization name so the team can identify who they're working with.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Product interest" icon="bed">
    The specific Mellanni products you're interested in — sheet sets, pillowcases, duvet covers, or a mix.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Estimated quantities" icon="boxes-stacking">
    The approximate number of units you need. All Mellanni bedding products are available for wholesale.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Use case" icon="briefcase">
    How you plan to use the products — hospitality, retail, gifting, healthcare, or another purpose.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Products available

All Mellanni bedding products are available for wholesale orders, including sheet sets, pillowcases, and duvet covers across the full range of colors and sizes.
