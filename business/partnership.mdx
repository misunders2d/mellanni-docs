Mellanni is an award-winning bedding brand with over 300,000 five-star reviews and millions of happy sleepers. If your brand, platform, or audience aligns with quality home goods and better sleep, Mellanni is open to exploring a range of partnership opportunities — from affiliate programs and influencer collaborations to co-marketing campaigns and brand partnerships.

## Types of partnerships

Mellanni considers a variety of partnership formats, including:

- **Affiliate programs** — Earn a commission by promoting Mellanni products to your audience.
- **Brand collaborations** — Co-create content or campaigns that reach new audiences together.
- **Co-marketing** — Joint promotions, newsletters, or social campaigns with aligned brands.
- **Influencer partnerships** — Sponsored content, gifting, and long-term creator relationships.

## How to apply

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the partnership page">
    Go to [mellanni.com/pages/partnership](https://mellanni.com/pages/partnership) to find the partnership inquiry form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit your inquiry">
    Fill out the form with details about yourself or your brand, your audience or platform, and the type of partnership you have in mind.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for a response">
    The Mellanni partnerships team will review your submission and follow up to explore the opportunity further.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  You can also reach the Mellanni brand team directly through social media. Mellanni is active on Instagram at [@mellanni_fine_linens](https://www.instagram.com/mellanni_fine_linens/), TikTok at [@mellanni](https://www.tiktok.com/@mellanni), and YouTube. These channels are a good way to introduce yourself before or alongside a formal inquiry.
</Note>
