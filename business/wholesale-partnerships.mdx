The Gladiator guide documents two business paths: wholesale and partnerships.

## Wholesale and bulk orders

Wholesale and bulk inquiries are for businesses or organizations that need large-volume orders.

| Buyer type | Example need |
| --- | --- |
| Hotels and resorts | Sheet and pillowcase rotation across rooms |
| Vacation rental operators | Standardized bedding for multiple properties |
| Hospitals and healthcare facilities | Large-volume bedding supply |
| Retail stores | Carrying Mellanni bedding products |
| Corporate gifting programs | Bulk gifting or employee programs |

## Wholesale inquiry checklist

Include these details when contacting Mellanni:

- Company or organization name.
- Product interest, such as sheet sets, pillowcases, duvet covers, or mixed bedding.
- Estimated quantities.
- Use case.
- Preferred sizes and colors.
- Timeline.

<Tip>
  The Gladiator wholesale page says the wholesale team responds in less than 24 hours to begin discussing terms.
</Tip>
